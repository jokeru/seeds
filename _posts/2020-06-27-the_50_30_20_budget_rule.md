## the 50/30/20 Budget Rule

Simple maths: `100% = 50% + 30% + 20%`

### 50% Need$
- housing 
- bills
- food
- transport

### 30% Want$
- hobbies
- going out
- shopping
- services (phone, gym, subscriptions)
- holidays

### 20% $aving$
- private pension
- deposits
- investments
